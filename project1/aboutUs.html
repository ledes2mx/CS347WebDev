About Us

We strive for perfection in our food and with our team. That is why we aquire our meats and produce from the best there is. When possible, we aquire our produce from local businesses to display the rich flavors of Virginia to all, from the people that live here locally or the ocasional visitors that pass through Harrisonburg.

Local Produce

  • Autumn Olive Farms - Waynesboro, VA
  • Buffalo Creek Farm - Lexington, VA
  • Mt. Crawford Creamery - Mount Crawford, VA
  • Season's Bounty - Harrisonburg, VA
  • Brooklyn Hills Ranch - Amherst, VA
  • Wildwood Gardens - Stanley, VA
  • DL Shipp Farm - Broadway, VA

Our Team

Our team is dedicated to bringing you the best and highest quality produce cooked in the highest and traditional way possible. We know that good cooking comes from the heart, so our staff is trained together to be able to answer any questions you may have about our food or the process in which we make our food with. That is why we choose to buy locally, to bring the best of Virginia's agriculture to your bellies.
