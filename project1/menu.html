El Cochinito

Tacos

Tacos come multiple varieties, all garnishes are optional or can be added.

Tacos de Asada
Grilled beef steak.
Garnishes include finely diced onions and cilantro
Tacos al Pastor
Pork marinated.
Garnishes include finely diced onions, cilantro, and pineapple.
Tacos de Canasta
Choose any of the three fillings: chicharron, potato, or beans.
No garnishes are included.
Tacos de Carnitas
Braized Pork, comes with many parts of the pig including the hock, belly, rinds, ribs, loin, leg, ect.
Garnishes include pickled jalapenos, thinly sliced carrots, and sauteed onions.
Cochinita Pibil
Underground roasted pig.
Garnish includes pickled red onions.
Garnishes include cilantro, pickled red onoins,
Seafood Tacos
Seafood tacos can either be catfish or shrimp. Either protein is fried.
Garnishes include red cabbage, cilantro, and avocado.
Tacos de Suadero
Fried beef cuts.
Garnishes include finely diced onions and cilantro.
Tacos de Birria
Roated marinated goat
Garnishes include cilantro and finely diced onions.
Tacos de Barbacoa
Roasted marinated lamb.
Garnishes include cilantro and finely diced onions.

Tortas

Tortas come in multiple varieties, all garnishes are optional. Bread is sourdough.

Torta de Carne Asada
Grilled beef steak.
Served with lettuce, tomato, avocado, pickled jalapeno, and mayonnaise.
Torta de Chorizo
Chorizo and beans.
Served with lettuce, tomato, avocado, pickled jalapeno, and sour cream.
Torta de Milanesa
Breaded steak.
Served with lettuce, tomato, avocado, pickled jalapeno, and sour cream.
Torta de Jamon
Ham or Turkey. Can either come warm or cold.
Served with lettuce, tomato, avocado, pickled jalapeno, and mayonnaise.
Torta de Pollo
Chicken braized in tomato sauce.
Served with lettuce, tomato, avocado, pickled jalapeno, and sour cream.

Entrees

Entrees are NOT tacos, you may ask for tortillas on the side. All additional items are optional.

Enchiladas
Enchiladas can be red, green, or mole poblano.
Comes with either lettuce or cabbage, sour cream, crumbling cheese, and avocado.
Huarache
Sliced flank steak.
Comes with either lettuce or cabbage, sour cream, crumbling cheese, and avocado.
Pozole
Pork and hominy soup.
Comes with cabbage, raddish, and tostadas.
Tamales
Either red pork tamales or green chicken tamales.
Comes with sour cream, crumbling cheese, and either lettuce or cabbage.

Vegitarian Options

All additional items are optional.

Vegitarian Tacos
Rice and beans.
Comes with lettuce, cheese, tomato, sour cream, and avocado.
Vegitarian Torta
Rice, beans, and cheese.
Served with lettuce, tomato, avocado, pickled jalapeno, and mayonnaise.
Quesadilla
Tortilla grilled cheese. Cheese is Oaxacan cheese.
